Mini Cooper SE 2019–2023

Practical range and charging estimates for the Mini Cooper SE.

Mini Range helps estimate remaining range and charging needs for the Mini Cooper SE using adjustable driving conditions and optional personal calibration.

Free. No accounts. No tracking.

I built Mini Range because I drive a Mini Cooper SE myself.

It is a wonderful small electric car, but its limited battery means that range matters in everyday use. The car’s own range estimate is useful, but it is mainly based on recent driving. It does not let you adjust for the trip you are about to make.

Mini Range started as a practical tool for my own use. The estimates are informed by real driving experience from my own Mini Cooper SE, together with transparent adjustments for conditions that affect consumption.

Since the app already exists, I see no reason not to share it with other Cooper SE drivers who may find it useful when estimating range or planning a longer drive.

What it does

Mini Range is designed for quick everyday estimates and simpler trip planning rather than full route optimisation.

Estimate remaining range

Enter the current battery level and get an approximate range estimate for the conditions you expect.

Plan a trip

Enter a planned distance and see whether the trip likely fits, or whether charging is probably needed.

Adjust driving conditions

Tune the estimate for road type, motorway speed, temperature, road surface, wind, tyres, and planning style.

Built for one car

The app is specifically aimed at the Mini Cooper SE 2019–2023, not electric cars in general or newer MINI EV models.

The app is private by design.

Mini Range does not collect personal information, driving data, location data, or usage analytics. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no third-party analytics services.

Mini Range is designed as a small independent utility app with a local-first and privacy-focused approach.

Feedback and suggestions are welcome.

Suggestions are especially useful from other Cooper SE drivers using the app in different conditions, such as cold weather, motorway driving, tyres, wind, or road surface.
