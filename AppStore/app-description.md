Mini Range

Mini Range helps Mini Cooper SE owners estimate remaining range and charging needs for everyday driving and longer trips.

The app is designed to provide practical and easy-to-understand estimates based on battery level and driving conditions, without the complexity of larger route planning systems.

Adjust conditions such as:

* road type
* motorway speed
* temperature
* road surface
* wind
* tyre efficiency

to see how they affect expected consumption and estimated range.

Features include:

* Remaining range estimates
* Charging distance estimates
* Simple trip planning
* Charging time estimates
* Charging strategy suggestions
* Personal calibration from logged trips
* On-device data storage with no account required

Mini Range is designed specifically for the Mini Cooper SE (2019–2023) and focuses on quick, transparent estimates with a comfortable safety margin for real-world driving.

The app provides approximate estimates only. Actual range and charging performance vary depending on weather, traffic, elevation, driving style, battery temperature, tyre pressure, battery condition, and other real-world factors.

Mini Range is an independent app and is not affiliated with MINI or BMW.
