# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is RangePilot an official MINI app?

No. RangePilot is an independent app and is not affiliated with MINI or BMW.

## Does the app collect driving data?

No. All trip data and settings remain locally stored on your device.

## Does the app require an account?

No. RangePilot works without accounts, subscriptions, or cloud services.

## How accurate are the estimates?

The estimates are approximate and depend on many factors, including:
- outdoor temperature
- driving speed
- road conditions
- wind
RangePilot does not currently model elevation or battery temperature.

## Why does motorway driving reduce range so much?

At higher speeds, aerodynamic drag increases energy consumption significantly. This is especially noticeable in smaller EVs such as the Mini Cooper SE.

## What does personal calibration do?

If you log trips in the app, the app can use your recent driving history to adjust the reference consumption for each driving mode and better match your own driving patterns and conditions.

## Does the app replace navigation apps?

No. RangePilot is designed as a simple range and charging companion, not a full navigation or route-planning system.

## Is my driving data uploaded anywhere?

No. The app is designed to work locally on your device without cloud storage or telemetry. Your logged trips are only stored locally within the app on your device.
